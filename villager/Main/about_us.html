About Our Panchayat

Kusumba Gram Panchayat is the cornerstone of local governance in our village, working tirelessly to ensure sustainable development and welfare of our community. Established under the Panchayati Raj system, we are committed to transparent, inclusive, and effective administration.

Panchayat Leadership

Gram Sevak

Our elected village head leads the panchayat, making key decisions for community development and welfare.

Ward Members

Dedicated representatives from different wards ensure every area's voice is heard in the panchayat.

Panchayat Secretary

Manages administrative functions and ensures smooth operation of panchayat activities.

Committee Members

Specialized committees oversee various aspects like education, health, and infrastructure.

Our Responsibilities

Tax Manager

Manages villager tax records and ensures proper tax collection.

Schemes and Meetings Manager

Oversees government schemes and organizes panchayat meetings.

Certificate Manager

Handles certificate applications, verification, and issuance processes.

Villager Manager

Manages villager records and registration processes.

Connect With Us

Visit us at the Panchayat Office during working hours (Monday to Saturday, 10 AM to 5 PM) for any assistance or queries. We are here to serve our community and work towards its betterment.
